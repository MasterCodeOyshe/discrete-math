## 📚 Study Materials & Topics
### Core Topics
- Mathematical problems of discrete nature.
- Discrete modeling (useful for CS/Information processing).
- **Matrix Calculations:** Note that methods often rely on matrix calculation.
- **Week 1 Topics:** Propositional logic, Reasoning, and Predicate logic.

### Materials
- Provided PDF lecture notes are sufficient (no need to buy books).
- **Reference Books:**
    - Richard Johnsonbaugh: *Discrete Mathematics* (5th/8th Ed).
    - Kenneth H. Rosen: *Discrete Mathematics and Its Applications* (7th Ed).
